Hi 👋 My name is Michael Portillo
=================================

Biomedical Engineering Student
------------------------------

I'm a fourth-year Biomedical Engineering honors student at the University of Texas at Austin specializing in molecular, cellular, and tissue biomechanics and mechanical design. My passions center on myoelectric prosthetics, powered exoskeletons, and humanoid robotics platforms, including interfaces that connect these devices to the human body. I'm particularly motivated by the challenge of integrating biological signals with electromechanical systems to create device that restore or enhance movement, independence, and quality of life. I'm eager to engage in research and industry work that involves designing, prototyping, and testing medical mechatronic devices--from advanced prosthetic hands to wearable assistive robotics. I have 5+ years of experience in SolidWorks, Onshape, and Fusion360, 5+ years of experience in mechanical design, prototyping, and documentation for competitive robotics, and 2+ years of experience in PCB design, KiCAD, and 3D printing. Additionally, I recently finished an internship at ClearCam Inc. as a Biomedical Engineer Intern.
